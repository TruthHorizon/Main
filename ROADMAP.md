# TruthHorizon Roadmap

The path to anchoring reality. This document outlines the technical and community milestones for the TruthHorizon protocol.

Everything is subject to change, we are flexible and things can change on the fly but this could be a potential flow to follow based on our goal.

## Phase 1: The Genesis Lens
*   **Chaos Material Research:** Community-driven testing of accessible "Chaos Materials" (UV resins, micro-particles, fibers).
*   **Hardware Design (v0.1):** Release of the first `.STL` 3D-printable universal clip-on mounts and MagSafe-compatible rings.
*   **Calibration Tool:** Development of a basic mobile utility to map a filter’s "Optical Fingerprint" (PUF - Physical Unclonable Function).

## Phase 2: The Trust Layer
*   **Blockchain Integration:** Deployment of smart contracts for immutable notarization of media hashes.
*   **Verification Portal:** A web interface allowing anyone to drag-and-drop a file to verify its physical origin and integrity.
*   **Beta Pilot:** Distributing 100 "Maker Kits" to independent journalists and activists for real-world field testing.

## Phase 3: Global Witness Network
*   **Hardware Certification:** Establishing open standards for third-party manufacturers to create "TruthHorizon-Ready" phone cases.
*   **API Ecosystem:** Enabling news organizations to integrate the TruthHorizon "Proof of Reality" badge into their CMS.
*   **Sovereign Governance:** Transitioning project leadership to a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) governed by the community.
